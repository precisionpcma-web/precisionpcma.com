PC repair

Laptop repair

Screen issues, charging problems, hardware failures — I'll assess what's going on and give you a straight answer on whether it's worth fixing.

Laptop troubleshooting

Maintenance & Other
Popular

Thermal repaste

Throttling under load, fans maxing out, running hot at idle — fresh thermal compound on the CPU, GPU, or VRM pads can drop temps by 10–20°C. I also do GPU VRM pad replacement, which most shops won't touch.

Dust removal & cleaning

Full disassembly and compressed air cleaning of fans, heatsinks, vents, and filters. Pairs well with a thermal repaste for the best cooling results.

Virus & malware removal

Pop-ups, browser hijacks, sluggish performance — full cleanup, rootkit scanning, and security hardening so it stays clean.
